Omics Informatics Table

About

This website contains a list of “omics” informatics platforms funded by ITCR grants. “Omics” platforms are those which work with several genomic or proteomic data types, such as sequence data. Each resource will have up to three links that navigate towards their main site, their documentation, and a list of their publications.
